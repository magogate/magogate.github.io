As expected, the weather becomes significantly warmer as one approaches the equator (0 Deg Latitude). More interestingly, however, is the fact that the southern hamisphere tends to be warmer this time of the year than the northern hamisphere. This may be due to the tilt of the earth at the time of the year this data was gathered.
